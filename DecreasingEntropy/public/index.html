Decreasing Entropy

The convolutedness of language in academia perfectly follows the 2nd Law of Thermodynamics—I'm here to reverse that, even if it seems physically impossible! How do I intend to do this? Simple—I'm reading and simplifying many different papers to help the general public better understand scholarly work without resorting to flashy and clickbaity popsci articles.
